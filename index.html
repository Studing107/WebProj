Plot Summary

SpongeBob SquarePants is a square-shaped yellow sponge who lives in a pineapple in Bikini Bottom. His pet is a sea snail named Gary who meows like a cat. SpongeBob enjoys catching jellyfish and works as the top chef at The Krusty Krab. His friends include Patrick Star and Sandy Cheeks. SpongeBob often creates trouble in the otherwise peaceful world, but he always manages to get out of trouble, only to create new problems.
